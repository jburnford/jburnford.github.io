About

I am an environmental and digital historian of Britain, Canada, and the British World during the long nineteenth century. My research explores the connections between rapid industrialization and urbanization in London and environmental transformations across the globe.

I am a co-editor of Historical Methods and ActiveHistory.ca, and a faculty member at the Historical GIS Lab.
