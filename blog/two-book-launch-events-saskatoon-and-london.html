Two Book Launch Events (Saskatoon and London)

April 02, 2018 | Categories: Books, Public Talk

My book West Ham and the River Lea is now out in paperback for $32.95 (Canadian). I am launching the paperback with two events in April. This first in Saskatoon on the 11th and the second in Stratford (West Ham) on the 25th. SASKATOON: Dave De Brou Memorial Lecture Wednesday, April 11th at 7:00 PM Frances Morrison Central Library Theatre, 311 23 St E, Saskatoon​ "Fixing the River Lea to Fix Unemployment in West Ham" I hope to have books for sale at the lecture and will at very least have flyers to order the book with a discount from UBC Press. LONDON: Book Launch in West Ham

Location: Stratford Library, 3 The Grove, London E15 1EL, UK (less than 10 minutes from the Stratford Underground Station).

Doors open: 5:30pm

Presentation: 6:30pm (a brief discussion of the book and a demo of interactive maps of 19th century factories in West Ham and London)

Books on sale at a discount: £19.99.

People who buy a book or show up with a book they’ve already bought will be invited on a walking tour of the Lower Lea Valley on Saturday April 27 with the author Jim Clifford and Ralph Ward (details to be announced).
